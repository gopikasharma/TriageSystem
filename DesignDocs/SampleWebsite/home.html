Process

How It Works

From symptom submission to confirmed appointment — in minutes.

1

Patient Submits Symptoms

Patient fills in the digital intake form with symptoms, pain level, and urgency via the Patient Portal.

2

AI Analyses & Scores

The AI engine processes the data within seconds and assigns a priority score (0–100) with confidence rating and department routing.

3

Nurse Validates

Nursing staff review the AI recommendation in the Triage Queue and may adjust the score based on clinical judgment.

4

Appointment Confirmed

An appointment is scheduled based on priority. The patient receives an SMS/email confirmation with all details.
